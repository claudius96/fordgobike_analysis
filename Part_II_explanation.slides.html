Part II - FordGo Bike sharing dataset exploration¶

by Muzimba Dingani¶

investigation overview¶

The objective of this presentation is to show and ephasize the key insights in the FordGo bike sharing system:

  1. more participants were not willing to share their rides.
  2. participants at the age of 26-41 participated the most and most of them were male.
  3. most people who participated were of year 1988.
  4. most participants were subscribers than customers.
  5. more men were willing to share their rides than than those who were not, more female were willing to share their rides than they weren't.

Dataset overview¶

This data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area.
